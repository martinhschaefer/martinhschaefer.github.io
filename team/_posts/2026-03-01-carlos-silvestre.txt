I hold a degree in Computer Engineering from the Universidad Europea de Madrid and a Joint Master's in Bioinformatics for Computational Genomics from Politecnico di Milano and University of Milan. My research focuses on developing deep learning and machine learning models to predict gene expression from CpG methylation profiles in metastatic brain cancer, leveraging model interpretation tools to uncover regulatory mechanisms underlying transcriptional changes during tumour evolution.
